! --- YouTube Anti-Adblock Bypass ---
! uBlock Origin handles YouTube via trusted scriptlets (trusted-replace-fetch-response,
! trusted-prevent-dom-bypass, trusted-rpnt). These scriptlets patch YouTube's player response
! so it never tries to serve ads — no detection is possible when done correctly.
!
! Problem: other filter lists in this merged set (EasyList, AdGuard, OISD, Hagezi, etc.)
! use traditional blocking on YouTube's ad infrastructure. YouTube's detection sees
! this traditional blocking even while the scriptlets are active, triggering the popup.
!
! Fix 1: $genericblock — disables generic (non-site-specific) blocking rules on YouTube pages.
!   Prevents OISD/Hagezi/EasyList domain-blocking rules from firing on youtube.com.
!   uBlock Origin's youtube.com-specific rules (with domain= option) are unaffected.
@@||youtube.com^$genericblock
@@||m.youtube.com^$genericblock
!
! Fix 2: $generichide — disables generic element hiding rules on YouTube pages.
!   Prevents generic ##.ad-container type rules from mutating YouTube's DOM
!   in ways that trigger YouTube's mutation-observer-based detection.
@@||youtube.com^$generichide
@@||m.youtube.com^$generichide
!
! Fix 3: Un-block YouTube's ad infrastructure domains from youtube.com pages.
!   These are the domains YouTube checks to verify its ad system is operational.
!   The scriptlet bypass makes YouTube never request ads — but if these are
!   hard-blocked before the scriptlet runs, it breaks the bypass mechanism.
@@||googleads.g.doubleclick.net^$domain=youtube.com,important
@@||securepubads.g.doubleclick.net^$domain=youtube.com,important
@@||pubads.g.doubleclick.net^$domain=youtube.com,important
@@||static.doubleclick.net^$domain=youtube.com,important
@@||pagead2.googlesyndication.com^$domain=youtube.com,important
@@||imasdk.googleapis.com^$domain=youtube.com,important
@@||s0.2mdn.net^$domain=youtube.com,important
@@||googlevideo.com^$domain=youtube.com,important
@@||www.youtube.com/api/stats/ads$xhr,domain=youtube.com,important
@@||www.youtube.com/pcs/activeview$xhr,domain=youtube.com,important
@@||www.youtube.com/pagead/^$domain=youtube.com,important
@@||www.youtube.com/get_video_info$xhr,domain=youtube.com,important
